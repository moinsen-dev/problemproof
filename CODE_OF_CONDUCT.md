# Code of Conduct

We want ProblemProof to support rigorous disagreement without personal attack.

Participants must:

- discuss claims, evidence, and implementation choices respectfully;
- distinguish facts, assumptions, hypotheses, and unknowns;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and unwanted disclosure
  of personal information;
- respect responsible-disclosure and moderation decisions;
- avoid spam, manipulation, and coordinated false validation signals.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions and restrict participation
when necessary to protect people, the project, or the integrity of its evidence.

Report conduct concerns privately to
[developer@moinsen.dev](mailto:developer@moinsen.dev). Reports will be handled
as confidentially as reasonably possible.
